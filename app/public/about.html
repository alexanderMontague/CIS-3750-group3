What Is BubbleT?

We are a team of Computer Science and Software Engineering students from the University of Guelph, and BubbleT is our team project for System Analysis and Design in Applications (CIS*3750, Winter 2019). BubbleT is an app that collects and displays salary records from Canada's public sector high earners.
